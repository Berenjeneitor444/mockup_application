1. What personal information do we collect?

The data we process is the data provided through the online pre-check-in functionality available at www.majestic-resorts.com.

The categories of data that we process for these purposes are the following:

- Identification data (name, surnames and DNI/passport) and contact information (address, email and telephone) of the reservation holder;

2. What will we process your data for and on what legal basis?

Online pre-check-in. The data provided through the online pre-check-in functionality will be processed to speed up the check-in process at the time of arrival at the destination hotel.

The use of online pre-check-in is optional and does not condition your entry to the hotel. If you do not use this functionality, you must fill in the entry part at the time of your arrival.

The treatment of the information necessary to pre-complete the entry part is based on the consent materialized through the use of this functionality.

Customization of the stay. We process the data related to the reason for your trip to process the personalization of treatment and services provided by the companies and hotels of the chain, for example, to congratulate you on your birthday or to take into account possible needs related to the reason you have expressed. for your stay.

This field is optional, not providing this information does not condition your accommodation or the services contracted with the hotel.

The treatment of the information on the reason for your stay is based on the consent materialized at the moment of indicating any of the options available for this purpose in the online pre-check-in process.

Sending of commercial communications and management of distribution lists: If you have requested or authorized it, your identification and contact data will be processed to send you communications regarding activities, products and tourist services of the MAJESTIC RESORTS chain.

This treatment is based on the consent given. Not giving or withdrawing your consent does not condition access to the Website or the provision of the requested services.

To manage our distribution lists, we classify the clients who have consented to receive our communications, based on their market or language. This treatment is based on our legitimate interest in segmenting the recipients of our communications. For the weighting of this interest with respect to their rights and freedoms, it has been determined that the treatment was compatible with the reasonable expectations of the interested parties, having given their consent to receive our commercial offers and constituting an accessory treatment for this purpose. Likewise, it has been considered that the treatment did not pose a significant threat to their privacy, as it consists of a simple classification based on objective criteria that do not allow new information to be inferred about the interested parties, excluding the performance of individualized commercial profiles, predictions or behavioral analysis.

At any time you can request your cancellation of the treatments for commercial purposes by activating the link provided for this purpose in our communications or by sending an email to: dpd@tychegroup.es

5. What are your rights?

You have the right to obtain confirmation of whether or not we are treating your personal data and, if so, to access them. You can also request that your data be rectified when it is inaccurate or that incomplete data be completed, as well as request its deletion when, among other reasons, the data is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

In certain circumstances, you may request the limitation of the processing of your data. In such a case, we will only treat the affected data for the formulation, exercise or defense of claims or with a view to protecting the rights of other people. Under certain conditions and for reasons related to your particular situation, you may also oppose the processing of your data. In this case, we will stop processing the data except for compelling legitimate reasons that prevail over your interests or rights and freedoms, or for the formulation, exercise or defense of claims.

Likewise, and under certain conditions, you may request the portability of your data so that they are transmitted to another data controller.

You can revoke the consent you have given for certain purposes, without affecting the legality of the treatment based on the consent prior to its withdrawal.

You also have the right to oppose the adoption of automated individual decisions that produce legal effects on you or significantly affect you in a similar way, when this right concurs in accordance with the provisions of article 22 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679.
