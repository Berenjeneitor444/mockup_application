NOTICE: DRUGS ARE PROHIBITED IN MEXICO

The possession, purchase, or use of drugs are illegal in Mexico. Do not be misled into believing that, because you are visiting, this is allowed or tolerated. It is strictly prohibited and will result in legal action by local police authorities.


Do not put yourself at risk. Drug dealers at beaches and nightclubs will get you into trouble with the law -- they are not your friends, but criminals. Avoid interacting with anyone selling to you in public places, including unlicensed vendors on the beach.


Enjoy a drug-free vacation to be a part of the solution.


This notice is to provide all visitors to the destination with the necessary information to be alert and make the right choices.
